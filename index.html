Frequently Asked Curiosities

Read details about our cooking practices, reservations, dietary options, and takeout.

Yes! While our traditional hand-pulled noodles are crafted from Hokkaido wheat flour, we offer a premium gluten-free glass noodle alternative for any of our signature broths. Please note that since we manufacture fresh wheat noodles on-site, trace amounts of cross-contamination can occur.
Our broth is slow-simmered at a controlled rolling boil for 24 continuous hours. This high-temperature, extended process allows the marrow and collagen in the pork bones to fully dissolve, creating an emulsified, creamy, milk-white soup base rich in collagen and loaded with depth of flavor.
We operate on a hybrid system. Our intimate wooden bar counter is kept open exclusively for walk-ins so travelers and ramen-enthusiasts can pop in anytime. However, our dining tables can be reserved online via the contact form or by calling us directly.
Absolutely! Our "Vegan Sesame Harvest" ramen is crafted from a rich base of ground roasted organic sesame paste, oat milk, and a vegetable dashi made from kombu seaweed and dried shiitake mushrooms. It comes with custom-tailored egg-free wave noodles.
Yes, we offer takeout. To ensure you experience the ramen at its peak texture, we pack our piping hot broth in specialized double-walled thermal containers, separate from the raw toppings and cooked noodles. You just combine them at home, heat for 30 seconds, and enjoy!
